# 1. BASIC TERMS (Start Here)

* **Perimeter** → total boundary length
* **Area** → space inside a shape
* **Volume** → space occupied in 3D
* **Diagonal** → line joining opposite corners
* **Radius (r)** → center to edge (circle)
* **Diameter (d)** → full width of circle = 2r

---

# 2. 2D SHAPES (FLAT SHAPES)

## Square

**Definition:** All sides equal, all angles = 90°

* Area = a²
* Perimeter = 4a
* Diagonal = √2 × a

---

## Rectangle

**Definition:** Opposite sides equal, all angles = 90°

* Area = l × b
* Perimeter = 2(l + b)
* Diagonal = √(l² + b²)

---

## Triangle

**Definition:** Shape with 3 sides

### General

* Area = ½ × base × height
* Perimeter = a + b + c

### Special Types

 **Equilateral Triangle (all sides equal)**

* Area = (√3/4) a²
* Height = (√3/2) a

 **Isosceles Triangle (2 sides equal)**

* Area = ½ × base × height

 **Right Triangle (90° angle)**

* Pythagoras: a² + b² = c²
* Area = ½ × base × height

 **Heron’s Formula (all triangles)**

* s = (a + b + c)/2
* Area = √[s(s−a)(s−b)(s−c)]

---

## Parallelogram

**Definition:** Opposite sides parallel and equal

* Area = base × height
* Perimeter = 2(a + b)

---

## Rhombus

**Definition:** All sides equal, diagonals perpendicular

* Area = (d₁ × d₂)/2
* Perimeter = 4a

---

## Trapezium (Trapezoid)

**Definition:** One pair of sides parallel

* Area = ½ × (a + b) × height

---

## Circle

**Definition:** All points at same distance from center

* Area = πr²
* Circumference = 2πr
* Diameter = 2r

 **Arc Length** = (θ/360) × 2πr
 **Sector Area** = (θ/360) × πr²

---

## Semi-circle

* Area = ½ πr²
* Perimeter = πr + 2r

---

# 3. 3D SHAPES (SOLIDS)

## Cube

**Definition:** All sides equal

* Volume = a³
* Surface Area = 6a²
* Diagonal = √3 × a

---

## Cuboid

**Definition:** Box shape

* Volume = l × b × h
* Surface Area = 2(lb + bh + hl)
* Diagonal = √(l² + b² + h²)

---

## Cylinder

**Definition:** Circular base + height

* Volume = πr²h
* Curved Surface Area = 2πrh
* Total Surface Area = 2πr(h + r)

---

## Cone

**Definition:** Circular base + pointed top

* Volume = (1/3)πr²h
* Slant Height (l) = √(r² + h²)
* Curved Surface Area = πrl
* Total Surface Area = πr(l + r)

---

## Sphere

**Definition:** Perfect round ball

* Volume = (4/3)πr³
* Surface Area = 4πr²

---

## Hemisphere

**Definition:** Half sphere

* Volume = (2/3)πr³
* Surface Area = 3πr²

---

## Prism

**Definition:** Same cross-section throughout

* Volume = Base Area × Height
* Surface Area = Lateral Area + 2 × Base Area

---

## Pyramid

**Definition:** Base + triangular faces meeting at top

* Volume = (1/3) × Base Area × Height

---

# 4. IMPORTANT THEOREMS

## Pythagoras Theorem

In a right triangle:
Hypotenuse² = Base² + Perpendicular²

---

## Trigonometry (Class 10–12)

For angle θ:

* sinθ = Perpendicular / Hypotenuse
* cosθ = Base / Hypotenuse
* tanθ = Perpendicular / Base
